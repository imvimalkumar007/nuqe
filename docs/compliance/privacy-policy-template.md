## 1. Who We Are

Nuqe Ltd ("Nuqe", "we", "us") provides a compliance management platform for financial services firms. This policy explains how we process personal data when you use our service.

## 2. Data We Collect

**Customer data (processed on behalf of our clients):**
We process personal data about the customers of our clients (financial services firms) including names, email addresses, telephone numbers, and complaint correspondence. We process this data as a **data processor** under instruction from our clients (who are data controllers). Individuals whose data is held should direct rights requests to their financial services firm in the first instance.

**User account data (our direct relationship):**
- Name and email address of staff users who access the platform
- Login timestamps and activity logs
- IP addresses (retained in access logs for 90 days)

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Delivering the complaint management service | Contractual necessity (Art.6(1)(b)) |
| Fraud prevention and security | Legitimate interests (Art.6(1)(f)) |
| Compliance with FCA and GDPR obligations | Legal obligation (Art.6(1)(c)) |
| Marketing communications (where consented) | Consent (Art.6(1)(a)) |

## 4. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Complaint case records | 7 years from case closure |
| Customer communications | 7 years from case closure |
| AI action logs | 2 years |
| Audit trail | 10 years |
| Staff access logs | 90 days |
| Marketing preferences | Until withdrawn |

## 5. Third Parties

We share personal data with the following categories of third party:
- **AI model providers** (Anthropic, OpenAI): to generate complaint response drafts. Data is not used for model training. Zero-retention agreements are in progress.
- **Cloud hosting** (Render): for database and application hosting.
- **Regulators**: FCA and ICO, where legally required.

We do not sell personal data.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request erasure, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing. Contact privacy@[domain] or submit a request via your financial services firm.

## 7. Security

We implement: AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive credentials, TLS 1.2+ in transit, JWT authentication, rate limiting, input validation, and immutable audit logging.
